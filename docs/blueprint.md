# **App Name**: MarketVerse AI

## Core Features:

- AI Product Recommendations: Suggest products tailored to individual users based on browsing history and purchase behavior. This will leverage a tool to consider and incorporate diverse products from our catalog.
- AI Ad Copy Generation: Enable marketing team workers to automatically generate social media ad copy using AI. This tool should allow modification for tone and targeting, prior to publication.
- Product Listing and Upload: Allow workers to upload product details (images, descriptions) with auto-generated tags and SEO text. Workers will only manage their own products.
- Flash Sales Countdown: Display a countdown timer for flash sales to create urgency and encourage purchases.
- Points and Rewards System: Implement a loyalty program where customers earn points on shopping, which can be redeemed for discounts (e.g., 500 points = 100 PKR discount).
- Order Tracking: Allow customers to track their orders in real-time.
- Worker Dashboard: Provide a dashboard for marketing team members to manage products, view sales performance, and generate ad copy.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Light, soft white background (#F9FAFB) to provide a clean and modern feel.
- Primary color: Indigo blue (#4F46E5) for buttons, links, and main call-to-action elements.
- Accent color: Amber (#F59E0B) to highlight flash sales and special offers, drawing user attention effectively.
- Secondary Color (for discounts & success states) → Emerald green `#10B981` - Used for “You saved 500 PKR” or rewards/points.
- Error/Alert Color → Red `#DC2626` - For “Out of stock” or urgent warnings.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines, and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text to give a modern yet readable aesthetic.
- Product cards: Use a white background with subtle shadows and rounded corners to elevate products visually.
- Use clear, modern icons that match the Indigo color palette, ensure visual clarity.
- Incorporate subtle animations (e.g., hover effects on buttons, smooth transitions) to enhance user engagement.
